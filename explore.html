Discover Croydon

Your Gateway to London & Beyond

Located on the historic Purley Way and just minutes from the former Croydon Airport, Sunday London Croydon Aerodrome Hotel is perfectly positioned for both business and leisure travellers. Enjoy easy access to Central London, Gatwick Airport, and a variety of local attractions.

Local Attractions

Things to Do Near the Hotel


Croydon Airport Visitor Centre

Just 0.3 miles away — celebrate the golden age of aviation at this fascinating heritage centre.

BOXPARK Croydon

Approx. 2 miles — vibrant street food, retail, and entertainment destination.

Addington Hills

3.6 miles — beautiful green space with stunning viewpoints over London.

Selhurst Park (Crystal Palace FC)

4.4 miles — home of Crystal Palace Football Club and major events.

Wimbledon

Approx. 8.2 miles — world-famous tennis and beautiful surroundings.

Central London

13.5 miles — easy access to Tower Bridge, museums, theatres and more.
