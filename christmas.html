Christmas at the Aerodrome Hotel

From festive lunches and private Christmas parties to corporate end-of-year celebrations, Sunday London Croydon Aerodrome Hotel offers flexible event spaces, seasonal menus, and accommodation options. Our historic Art Deco setting and the grand Amy Johnson Suite make it the perfect venue for both intimate gatherings and larger celebrations.
