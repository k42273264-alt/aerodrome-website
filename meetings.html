Meetings & Events

Historic Venue, Modern Facilities

Sunday London Croydon Aerodrome Hotel offers five versatile meeting and event spaces, ideal for conferences, training sessions, board meetings, private dining and celebrations. Our largest suite, the Amy Johnson Suite, can accommodate up to 200 guests for receptions.
