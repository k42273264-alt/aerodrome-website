Our Story

Sunday London Croydon Aerodrome Hotel

1928: A Landmark is Born

Originally built as part of the iconic Croydon Airport complex, the Aerodrome Hotel opened in July 1928. It served as a pioneering hotel directly connected to one of the world's most important early international airports.

Legendary Connection

Aviation pioneer Amy Johnson stayed at the hotel before her record-breaking solo flight to Australia. Today, the hotel proudly carries forward this rich aviation heritage while offering modern comfort and excellent hospitality.
