A Historic Aviation Landmark

A celebrated aviation landmark since 1928, Sunday London Croydon Aerodrome Hotel blends Art Deco character with contemporary comfort. Set on the A23 London-Gatwick route, the hotel offers 109 bedrooms, flexible meeting and event spaces, relaxed dining, landscaped gardens and convenient transport links for business and leisure guests.

Relaxed & Practical Stays

Our 109 bedrooms are designed to provide a relaxed and practical stay. Whether visiting for business, leisure, a group event or a celebration, you’ll enjoy comfortable beds, modern amenities, and the unique charm of a historic Art Deco hotel.
